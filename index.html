About Us

Kabete Friends Church is a vibrant community dedicated to nurturing faith, fostering fellowship, and serving those in need. Rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, we believe in the power of love, unity, and outreach to make a difference in our world. Whether through worship, Bible study, or community programs, we aim to create a space where all individuals can grow spiritually and contribute to the well-being of society. Join us as we journey together in faith, with open hearts and a mission to spread God's love.
